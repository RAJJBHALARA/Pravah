Our Story

Two Cities. One Dream. Endless Flow.

It started with a phone call.

Two brothers — separated by 200 kilometres, by two different cities, two different universities — talking late into the night the way brothers do. About life. About ambition. About something that had always bothered them both.

Why does luxury have to feel so out of reach?

One was buried in textbooks at Marwadi University, Rajkot. The other was pushing boundaries at NFSU, Gandhinagar. Different paths. Same restlessness. Because somewhere between lectures and deadlines, they'd both noticed the same thing — a world where a single bottle of fine fragrance costs what a student earns in a month. A world where luxury was a velvet rope, not an open door.

That night, they decided to change it.

Pravah was born — not from a boardroom or a billion-dollar lab — but from two hostel rooms, a shared obsession, and the unshakeable belief that everyone deserves to feel extraordinary.

The name means flow — like a river that doesn't ask permission before it fills every corner with something beautiful. Unstoppable. Natural. Free. That's what a great scent does. It doesn't care if you're wearing a designer suit or a college hoodie. It just flows — and suddenly, you feel like the most powerful version of yourself.

"Luxury is not a price tag. It is a feeling.
And that feeling belongs to everyone."

Today, Pravah is more than a perfume brand. It is a movement — crafted by two brothers who refused to let geography, or budget, or "that's just how it is" stop them. One bottle at a time. One person at a time.

The Pravah Fragrance Guide

Master the Art of
Layering Perfumes

Learn where to spray, how to layer, and the secrets to making your Pravah scent last all day.

Where to Apply

Pulse points are warm areas where blood vessels are close to the skin. The heat helps diffuse and amplify your fragrance throughout the day.

🤚

Inner Wrists

The classic spot. Spray and let it dry — never rub your wrists together, it crushes the top notes.

💫

Neck & Throat

One spray on each side of your neck. The warmth here projects your scent as you move.

👂

Behind the Ears

A subtle, intimate spot. Perfect for close conversations and for scent that lasts.

👔

Chest & Collar

A spray on your chest or shirt collar creates a scent "cloud" that follows you everywhere.

The 3-Step Layering Method

1

Moisturize First

Apply an unscented body lotion or petroleum jelly on pulse points. Hydrated skin holds fragrance 2-3x longer than dry skin.

2

Spray Your Perfume

Hold the bottle 6 inches away and spray 2-3 pumps on pulse points. Let it air dry — don't rub. Less is more.

3

Lock It In

For maximum longevity, spray once on your clothes (inner elbow of a jacket or scarf). Fabric holds scent for hours longer than skin.

How to Store Your Perfume

🌡️

Avoid Heat

Keep your Pravah bottle away from direct sunlight and heat. Room temperature or a drawer is ideal.

💧

No Humidity

The bathroom is the worst spot for perfume. Moisture breaks down fragrance molecules faster.

📦

Keep the Box

Store your bottle in its original box when not in use. It protects from light and temperature changes.
