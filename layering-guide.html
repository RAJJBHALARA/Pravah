The Pravah Fragrance Guide

Master the Art of
Layering Perfumes

Learn where to spray, how to layer, and the secrets to making your Pravah scent last all day.

Where to Apply

Pulse points are warm areas where blood vessels are close to the skin. The heat helps diffuse and amplify your fragrance throughout the day.

🤚

Inner Wrists

The classic spot. Spray and let it dry — never rub your wrists together, it crushes the top notes.

💫

Neck & Throat

One spray on each side of your neck. The warmth here projects your scent as you move.

👂

Behind the Ears

A subtle, intimate spot. Perfect for close conversations and for scent that lasts.

👔

Chest & Collar

A spray on your chest or shirt collar creates a scent "cloud" that follows you everywhere.

The 3-Step Layering Method

1

Moisturize First

Apply an unscented body lotion or petroleum jelly on pulse points. Hydrated skin holds fragrance 2-3x longer than dry skin.

2

Spray Your Perfume

Hold the bottle 6 inches away and spray 2-3 pumps on pulse points. Let it air dry — don't rub. Less is more.

3

Lock It In

For maximum longevity, spray once on your clothes (inner elbow of a jacket or scarf). Fabric holds scent for hours longer than skin.

How to Store Your Perfume

🌡️

Avoid Heat

Keep your Pravah bottle away from direct sunlight and heat. Room temperature or a drawer is ideal.

💧

No Humidity

The bathroom is the worst spot for perfume. Moisture breaks down fragrance molecules faster.

📦

Keep the Box

Store your bottle in its original box when not in use. It protects from light and temperature changes.

Pravah Combos That Layer Beautifully

Try these combinations for a scent that's uniquely you.

Bare Accord + Blush Hour

✦ Clean Romance

The clean musk base of Bare Accord amplifies the soft florals of Blush Hour. Result: a skin-close romantic scent that smells expensive.

Clear Theory + Golden Resin

✦ Fresh Luxury

Open with Clear Theory's citrus freshness, then the amber warmth of Golden Resin takes over. Day-to-night in one combo.

Bare Accord + Too Late Tonight

✦ Addictive Depth

The skin musk of Bare Accord softens Too Late's intensity, creating a seductive scent that's dark without being overwhelming.

Blush Hour + Golden Resin

✦ Warm Floral

Soft petals meet rich amber. This combo is wedding-ready — elegant, warm, and unforgettably beautiful.
